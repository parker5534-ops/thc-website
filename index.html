About the Community

Gaming-first.
Community always.

T!H$C started as a squad. It grew into something more — a place where you actually want to log on, not just to game, but to talk, vibe, and connect with people who get it.

Built in Houston, expanded to Discord in 2024. We focus on real community — not just a list of channels with nobody in them. Every space in T!H$C has a purpose, and every member has a place. Whether you're here to grind ranked, find a squad, or just hang out in voice with good people — this is your spot.
